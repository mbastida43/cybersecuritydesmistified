CyberSecurity Demystified

CyberSecurity Demystified uses everyday analogies to explain complex security, distinguishing between Information Security and specialized Cybersecurity roles. The guide outlines key concepts, including the CIA Triad, threat-vulnerability risk, and the ethical hacker spectrum. It maps the Cyber Kill Chain attack lifecycle and highlights core defensive principles like Zero Trust and Least Privilege. Finally, the material emphasizes a "Secure by Design" approach and outlines collaborative testing strategies through Red and Blue teaming.
